Hazel's Story

When I first met Jacob Harris in High School I was a little intimidated. He was (and still is to this day ) a loud and proud man who could easily grab the attention of an entire room. The way he walked, the way he talked the way he smiled. To be honest with you it took my breath away and I'm still trying to catch my breath to this day lol. In all seriousness though, time split us apart. We had to find out what love truly was and how it felt to be misunderstood and mishandled in relationships to truly grasp the love that we have for one another. On Thursday May 28,2021 Jacob asked me to marry him. After some laughter and some tears I said "Yes!".

Jacob's Story
