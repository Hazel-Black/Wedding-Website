Hazel's Story

When I first met Jacob Harris in High School I was a little intimidated. He was (and still is to this day ) a loud and proud man who could easily grab the attention of an entire room. The way he walked, the way he talked the way he smiled. To be honest with you it took my breath away and I'm still trying to catch my breath to this day lol. In all seriousness though, time split us apart. We had to find out what love truly was and how it felt to be misunderstood and mishandled in relationships to truly grasp the love that we have for one another. I can't really tell you what I would do without this amazing man in my life but I can say what I will do. I will be there for him when he needs a shoulder to cry on or someone to vent to. I will be there when he celebrates his biggest wins and I will cheer him on. I will encourage him when he doubts himself and push him to always do his best. But most importantly I will love him, I will love him forever and always.
